About Us



Brightwayz is a competency building institute and has been known for its innovative and customized approach for education sector.

Brightwayz is catering major sector of graduation to postgraduate providing various bunch of innovative service to create effective and efficient workforce to corporate sector.

Our Team and mentors are majorly from VJTI,SP Jain and having core domain experience and have core vision of innovative current education system by imparting core domain knowledge with well defined mixture of corporate.

Brightwayz Team is consistently defining systematic and focussed approach to all graduate students with which they can enhance corporate skills.These skills which enable then to undestand the conditions prevailing in the corporate sector and will make them comeptent enough.

Brightwayz team is believing on holding hands and guiding them to nurture strength and enhance respective skills
