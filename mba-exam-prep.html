MBA Exam Preparation Module

Brightwayz MBA Preparation Module

BCS - Brigtwayz MBA preparation Module is specifically designed for specific students who would like to do an MBA from TOP MBA Colleges.

MBA is known as Masters In Business Administration and can done with following 5 ways
1) Full time –2 years - MMS,MBA and PGDM
2) Part Time- 3 years – MMM.MFM,MHRM
3) Distance
4) Executive MBA
5) Correspondence MBA


Out of this, Full time MBA is attracting major value from all MBA aspirants. Full time MBA bifurcated into following Degrees

     1) MMS- Masters in management studies – Mumbai University
     2) MBA- Other than Mumbai university
     3) PGDM- Post Graduate diploma in Business Administration- AICTE Approved

To pursue admission into above mentioned courses, every students needs to pass one defined common entrance test successfully which are as mentioned –

     1) CAT
     2) CMAT
     3) CET
     4) MAT/ATMA
     5) SNAP
     6) NMAT
     7) XAT
     8) GMAT- Foreign MBA

With this exams you can qualify for an MBA and with the sub sequent process of Group Discussion and Personal interview, you can convert your call into final admission.
